My favorite recipe: brownies

Ingridients

  • 110g dark chocolate
  • 110g butter
  • 110g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 60g flour

Instructions

  1. Melt butter, sugar and chocolate in a pot
  2. Let the chocolate mixture cool a little
  3. Mix in the eggs one by one
  4. Mix in the flour and pour the mixture in a cake pan
  5. Bake at 150C for ca 40Minutes
